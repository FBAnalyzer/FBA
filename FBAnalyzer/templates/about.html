What is Floorball Scanner?





Floorball Scanner gives you insight into your team's performance in real-time during the game. You are able to see how each line performs and take actions based on the data.






Shotmap


Map easily your and opponents shots and get a picture of how shots are distributed on the field.





Advanced statistics


See deeper into the game result with advanced statistics, such as expected goals (xG) and ball possessions of each line individually. With advanced statistics provided by Floorball Scanner you are able to make better and more informed decisions also during game.





Intuitive visualizations


Sophisticated graphs and pictures help you form a better understanding of how your team and lines are doing against the opponent.
